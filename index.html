FlexPass

FlexPass is a digital hallpass system, meant to make communication between teachers more seamless, and better enable administrators to locate students in the event of an emergency. It allows students to digitaly request a hall pass, mainly during their FLEX period (Study hall), to go to various locations within the school. It is being developed in Python, by the members of Tech Club.
